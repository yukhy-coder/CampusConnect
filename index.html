Vision, Mission, Goal & Outcome

Our future is shaped by the principles that guide us todayâ€”our Vision, Mission, Goals, and Outcomes, which reflect our commitment to excellence, inclusivity, and sustainable development.

VISION

CTU as a Premier, Inclusive, Globally-Recognized Research and Innovation Smart, Community Responsive and Sustainable Technological University.

MISSION

The University shall primarily provide leading-edge degree programs, innovative professional, entrepreneurial, and technical instruction as well as research and extension programs that address both the needs of the region and the nation in the context of the global knowledge economy, Fourth Industrial Revolution, and sustainability.

GOALS

By the end of 2024, CTU will be globally recognized for its quality. Specifically, it will:

  • Obtain a Philippine university system status with a CHED SUC Level V recognition or equivalent.
  • Be ranked among the Top 10 Universities in the Philippines.
  • Be included in the list of internationally recognized ranking of universities.
  • Pursue efforts to pave way for the development of new programs.
  • Achieve world-class performance and excellence in total quality management.
  • Lead innovations in the Fifth Industrial Revolution.

OUTCOME

  1. Relevant, effective, and quality education for sustainable growth.
  2. Access to quality education for the underprivileged students.
  3. Relevant research for economic, environmental, and sustainable development.
  4. Expanded community engagement.
  5. Effective and efficient management of resources.
